## Objective

Make duplicate-arrival alerts dependable on a real Android device without regressing Jupiter's existing file-manager, dedup, index, Automation, storage or safe-delete behavior. The result must compare a newly downloaded image against the existing index promptly, retain a retryable failure rather than advancing beyond it, and deliver a previously blocked alert after Android permits delivery.
